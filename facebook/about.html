Welcome to PICT !

Pune Institute of Computer Technology (PICT) stands as a beacon of excellence in the field of computer education, deeply rooted in Pune's educational landscape since its establishment in 1983. Affiliated with Savitribai Phule Pune University, PICT has consistently upheld a tradition of academic brilliance, innovation, and holistic development.
With a diverse array of undergraduate and postgraduate programs in computer science, information technology, electronics & telecommunication, mechanical engineering, and civil engineering, PICT caters to the dynamic demands of the industry while nurturing the intellect and character of its students.
