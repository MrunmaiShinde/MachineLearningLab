Welcome to PICT !

Pune Institute of Computer Technology (PICT) stands as a beacon of excellence in the field of computer education, deeply rooted in Pune's educational landscape since its establishment in 1983. Affiliated with Savitribai Phule Pune University, PICT has consistently upheld a tradition of academic brilliance, innovation, and holistic development.
With a diverse array of undergraduate and postgraduate programs in computer science, information technology, electronics & telecommunication, mechanical engineering, and civil engineering, PICT caters to the dynamic demands of the industry while nurturing the intellect and character of its students.

About PICT

Vision

Our vision at PICT is to emerge as a globally recognized center of excellence in computer education and research, fostering innovation, leadership, and societal impact. We aspire to empower students with cutting-edge knowledge and skills, instilling in them a passion for lifelong learning, and nurturing a culture of creativity, collaboration, and ethical leadership.

Mission

At PICT, our mission is to provide a transformative learning experience that equips students with the technical proficiency, critical thinking abilities, and professional competencies to excel in the dynamic field of computer science and engineering. We are committed to fostering a vibrant academic community that promotes interdisciplinary research, industry engagement, and social responsibility. Through our rigorous curriculum, state-of-the-art infrastructure, and dedicated faculty, we strive to cultivate a spirit of innovation, entrepreneurship, and global citizenship among our students, preparing them to address the challenges and opportunities of the digital age.
